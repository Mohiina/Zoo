Storm is our energetic Siberian Husky, bred for endurance and strength in harsh arctic conditions. These remarkable dogs were originally developed by the Chukchi people of Siberia for sledding and companionship. Huskies are known for their incredible stamina, thick double coat, and distinctive blue or multi-colored eyes. Storm loves to run and play in the snow, showcasing the breed's natural athleticism and friendly nature.
