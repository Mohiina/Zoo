Onyx is our magnificent orca whale, also known as a killer whale. These apex predators are actually the largest members of the dolphin family and are highly intelligent marine mammals. Orcas are known for their complex social structures, sophisticated hunting techniques, and remarkable communication abilities. Onyx demonstrates the species' intelligence through interactive behaviors and problem-solving.
