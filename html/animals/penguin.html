Pip and Pebble are our adorable Emperor penguin pair, the largest of all penguin species. These remarkable birds can dive deeper than any other bird, reaching depths of over 500 meters. Emperor penguins are incredible parents, with males incubating eggs on their feet for two months in the harshest winter conditions on Earth. Pip and Pebble demonstrate the species' social nature and teamwork.
